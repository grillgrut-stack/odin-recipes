Lasagna

Description

A delicious cheesy lasagna using an old recipes from the 1800s. This will leave you falling in love with the 4 cheese style from back then.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Pasta
  • Love

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients into a pan
  2. Add some love into the mix
  3. Stir it all together so it mixes nicely
  4. Put it in the oven at 250F for 20 mins
  5. Enjoy!
